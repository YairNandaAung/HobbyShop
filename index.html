Gunpla Types

HG Kits

High Grade (HG)They are a great size and have plenty of detail, HG models are the go-to for most builders. These 1/144 scale kits are budget-friendly, easy to construct, and offer the biggest variety across the whole Gunpla range Gundam.

RG Kits

Real Grade (RG)RG Gundam kits provide a delightful balance of complexity and compactness. They unite the intricate skeletal inner frame characteristic of the Master Grade models with the convenient 1/144 scale of the High-Grade kits.
